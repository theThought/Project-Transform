Line definitions
Start on their 1st line in the advanced/metastatic setting = at this visit, you started these patients on their first treatment after a diagnosis of advanced/metastatic (progressed to distant sites) tumor, irrespective of any other treatments they received prior to progressing to metastatic disease (do not account for adjuvant/neo-adjuvant treatments in earlier stages)

Start on their 2nd or later lines in the advanced/metastatic setting = at this visit, you started these patients on their 2nd or later lines of treatment in the advanced/metastatic (progressed to distant sites) setting, including regimen additions, subtractions, beginning of maintenance therapy and/or a completely new regimen (do not account for adjuvant/neo-adjuvant treatments in earlier stages)


Note: a change in dosage or schedule does NOT count as a change in regimen/line of therapy
